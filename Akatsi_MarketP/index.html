History & Heritage

Akatsi was founded by the great hunter Adela Letsa Korba I in the 16th century, whose ancestors hailed from Anloga.

The market land was acquired from the Letsa Korba Family, who are the Overlords of Akatsi.

Community News & Projects

Project Spotlight: The 24-Hour Market Initiative

Akatsi is transforming into a 24-hour trading hub. To facilitate this, the local assembly has commissioned modern security outposts and lighting infrastructure to ensure high safety standards for late-night traders.
